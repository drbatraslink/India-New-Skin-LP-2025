Acne

Acne

Acne occurs when hair follicles are clogged with oil, dead skin cells or bacteria. Genetics, fluctuating hormonal levels, oily personal care products can cause acne. Untreated acne leaves lifelong scars.

Warts

Warts are viral skin infections and all type of warts is contagious. It can take as long as two to six months to develop after exposure to virus. The warts can spread from person to person or different parts of body through direct contact with warts when people pick at their warts bite their nails and do not properly wash their hands.
