Psoriasis

Psoriasis

Psoriasis is a chronic skin condition characterised by inflamed, red, raised areas with silvery scales. It often worsens in winter and can be triggered by trauma or stress.

At Dr Batra's®, our expert skin doctors and advanced skin clinic offer holistic, homeopathic care for psoriasis, achieving a 94.3% success rate in treatment and long-term skin improvement.

Vitiligo

Vitiligo

Vitiligo is an autoimmune skin condition where melanin-producing cells stop working or are destroyed, leading to visible white patches on the skin. It often affects self-esteem and can cause emotional distress.

At Dr Batra's®, our expert skin doctors and advanced skin clinic offer holistic, root-cause-based treatment for vitiligo. With a 91% success rate, we use safe homeopathy and personalised care to naturally restore skin tone and confidence.

Eczema

Eczema

Eczema is a chronic skin condition marked by itchy and inflamed skin due to triggers like dust mites, pet fur, pollen, soaps, detergents, and stress.

At Dr Batra's®, we achieve a 96.1% success rate in eczema treatment with holistic, homeopathic care, provided by expert doctors at our advanced skin clinic.

Acne

Acne

Acne occurs when hair follicles are clogged with oil, dead skin cells or bacteria. Genetics, fluctuating hormonal levels, and oily personal care products can cause acne. Untreated acne leaves lifelong scars.

Our qualified doctors at Dr Batra's® skin clinic treat acne from the root with safe, personalised homeopathy.

Warts

Warts

Warts are viral skin infections and all types of warts are contagious. It can take two to six months to develop after exposure to the virus. Warts can spread from person to person or to different parts of the body through direct contact, especially when people pick at warts, bite their nails, or do not properly wash their hands.

At Dr Batra's®, our experienced doctors at the skin clinic treat warts safely and effectively with homeopathy.

lichen planuss

Lichen Planus

The causes of lichen planus are unknown. It can affect many areas of the skin, nails, scalp, genitals, and mouth. It usually appears as purplish, itchy, flat bumps that develop over several weeks.

At Dr Batra's®, the approach to lichen planus includes complete homeopathic medical history taken by our expert doctors at the skin clinic, ensuring rapid healing with minimal recurrence.

Fungal Infection

Fungal Infection

Fungi are common in the environment and spread through direct skin contact or contaminated items like soil and towels. People with weak immune systems, diabetes, or those undergoing treatments like antibiotics are more vulnerable.

At Dr Batra's®, our skilled doctors at the skin clinic treat fungal infections from the root with safe, long-term solutions.
